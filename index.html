WHO WE ARE

The Vuslat Foundation is a global initiative that brings together a community of artists, storytellers, thought leaders and changemakers to promote the ideal that when we listen generously, when we are curious about thoughts and beliefs other than our own, we move closer toward genuine unity.
DISCOVER

OUR MISSION

Our purpose is to create a transformative movement that makes generous listening to one’s self, to others and to nature an essential part of being connected to the world and each other.
READ MORE
OUR
MANIFESTO
We don’t listen anymore. Somewhere along the way, we’ve become prisoners of our prejudice. Intolerant of voices different from our own. Unwilling to listen. To nature. To each other. To ourselves. It’s time we came to understand that we can change things. That when we listen with our hearts, we learn that our differences are our most sacred treasures. Let us find the truth in our hearts, and the hearts of one another, for there we discover our unity and our uniqueness.
GENEROUS
LISTENING
When we listen with purpose, we put aside our own thoughts and opinions to genuinely understand why others think and believe as they do. Generous listening is deep listening. It is undistracted. It’s muting our ego and opening our minds to another perspective.

THE MEANING
OF VUSLAT

The foundation gets its name from the perennial concept of “vuslat,” which describes both the journey and the bliss we experience when we reunite with the essence or ourselves and others. It is a moment of epiphany when one simultaneously recognizes the oneness of ourselves while appreciating the uniqueness of all.

WHAT WE DO
With their creativity, talent, cultural connections and unique global perspectives, a select collective of Artists and Storytellers manifest the concept of generous listening through their work, transcending stereotypes. Concurrently and in concert with our Artists and Storytellers, we collaborate with some of the world’s foremost Thought Leaders and Change Makers.
